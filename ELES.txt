ASTEROID IMPACT ANALYSIS & EXTINCTION EVENT SIMULATOR PROJECT

=== SECTION 1: 2KM METAL ASTEROID IMPACT ===

Impact Parameters:
- Size: 2 km diameter
- Composition: Iron-nickel metal
- Density: ~8000 kg/m³
- Velocity: ~20 km/s
- Mass: ~3.35 × 10¹³ kg
- Impact Energy: ~6.7 × 10²¹ J (~1.6 million megatons TNT)

Immediate Effects:
- Crater: 30-50 km diameter, ~10 km depth
- Shockwave: Flattens everything within hundreds of km
- Winds: Up to 1000 km/h near ground zero
- Earthquakes: Magnitude 9+ globally
- Tsunamis: 100+ meter waves if ocean impact

Global Consequences:
- Firestorms across continents
- Global temperature drop up to 10°C
- Months-long darkness from atmospheric debris
- Agricultural collapse
- Mass extinction event (similar to dinosaur extinction)

=== SECTION 2: APOPHIS ASTEROID ANALYSIS ===

Apophis Specifications:
- Diameter: ~370 meters
- Mass: ~5 × 10¹⁰ kg
- Impact Energy: ~1 × 10¹⁹ J (~2,400 megatons TNT)
- Status: Will NOT impact Earth (2029, 2036, or 2068)

Hypothetical Impact Effects:
- Crater: 4-6 km diameter
- Destruction radius: 50 km structural collapse
- Regional catastrophe (not global extinction)
- Millions at risk if populated area hit
- Economic damage in trillions

=== SECTION 3: LARGER THAN CHICXULUB IMPACT ===

Chicxulub Comparison (Dinosaur Extinction Event):
- Original size: 10-12 km diameter
- Energy: ~10²³ J (~100 million megatons)
- Result: 75% species extinction

Larger Impact Scenario (15-25 km):
- Energy: 10-20x greater than Chicxulub
- Crater: 200-500 km diameter
- Global temperature drop: 20-40°C
- Extinction rate: >90% of all species
- Human survival: Extremely unlikely

=== SECTION 4: PROJECT PROPOSAL ===

Project Name: E.L.E.S. (Extinction-Level Event Simulator)

Simulation Categories:
1. Astronomical Events
    - Asteroid impacts
    - Gamma-ray bursts
    - Solar flares
    - Nearby supernovae

2. Geological Events
    - Supervolcanoes (Yellowstone, Toba)
    - Massive flood basalts
    - Tectonic mega-shifts

3. Climate Events
    - Runaway greenhouse effect
    - Snowball Earth scenarios
    - Ocean anoxia events

4. Biological Events
    - Pandemic outbreaks
    - Ecosystem collapse
    - Invasive species

5. Anthropogenic Events
    - Nuclear warfare
    - AI-related risks
    - Biotechnology disasters

6. Speculative Events
    - Alien encounters
    - Physics anomalies
    - Space-time disruptions
